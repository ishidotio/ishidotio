• JUN 6 — rOtring 600
    As far as I am concerned, the rOtring 600 mechanical pencil is the finest drawing instrument ever created. And as a machinist apprentice, it is an absolute work of art. My leads are 0.5mm HB.
  • JUN 3 — HP-12C
    The HP-12C is the quintessential calculating instrument. My first HP calculator was a used HP-28C. It was love at first sight! Soon afterwards, I got a brand new HP-28S. It had a very distinctive smell, like a new car. Today, I got a new HP-12C, but it has no fragrance. Nostalgia.
